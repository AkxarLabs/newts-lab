Welcome to the Vivarium

Your lab, rendered as a living world. Here's how to read it.

🏛️Rooms are stagesEach room is a lifecycle stage — Incubator → Study → Lab → Writing → Archive, plus the Margins for parked/killed work. An idea/project sits in the room for its stage.
🦎Buddies are the workEach creature is an idea/project (coloured by project) or a working agent. The little pip on an agent shows its role. Newt, bottom-centre, is the orchestrator — and your command handle.
🔍Click to dive inClick a room (or a project buddy) to zoom into it and watch its crew move. Click an agent to open its inspector — what it's doing now + its full action log — and ⊙ follow it.
⌁Stay in the loopThe ◔ bell shows what needs you (gates, escalations). ⌕ (or /) is the command palette. ⚙ holds settings, theme & the lab's knowledge. Gates 1 & 2 you can approve here; Gate 3 is always done in a session.

Want to see it move without a live session? — a synthetic living lab to explore. Light / dark lives in ⚙.
